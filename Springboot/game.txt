1. Opening Scene – The Abandoned Place
Setting: A foggy, abandoned industrial site (rusted fences, broken signs, eerie silence).

Characters:

MC (Main Character): cautious, observant.

Friend: curious, reckless.

Action:

2. The Gathering
Scene: MC returns to a safe spot (campfire or garage).

Characters: MC + 3 Bros (each with distinct personalities: strategist, muscle, joker).

Action:

MC explains what happened.

The group decides to go find the missing friend.

Dialogue builds tension: “If he’s in there, we’re not leaving him behind.”

3. The Search
Scene: The 4 set out together.

Gameplay Element:

Exploration mechanics (flashlights, map fragments, eerie sound design).

Environmental storytelling (scratched walls, abandoned notes hinting at danger).

4. The House Appears
Scene: A mysterious house materializes in the restricted zone (could be supernatural or hidden until triggered).

Action:

Strange forces begin damaging the house (cracks forming, windows shattering, unseen pressure).

The group realizes they must fortify the entrance.

5. Defense Mechanics
Gameplay Animation Ideas:

Use ropes, planks, barrels, chains to block doors and windows.

Quick-time events: tying knots, hammering boards, dragging furniture.

Dynamic animation: house shakes, debris falls, ropes strain under pressure.

6. Suspenseful Cliffhanger
Final Beat:

As the house stabilizes, a shadowy figure appears inside.

Is it the missing friend… or something else?

Screen fades to black → “To Be Continued…”

🎨 Animation Style Notes
Mood: Dark, suspenseful, with glowing highlights (flashlights, sparks).

Camera Work:

Wide shots for exploration.

Close-ups during defense mechanics (hands tying ropes, sweat dripping).

Sound Design:

Echoing footsteps, creaking wood, muffled voices.

Sudden silence before big reveals.
